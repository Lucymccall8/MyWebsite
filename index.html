About Me
Hi, I'm Lucy! I've just graduated university with a distinction in a Computer Science Masters Degree. I'm a full stack developer and my main interests are Mobile Development, Python, and JavaScript. I especially enjoy using Flutter to create useful apps, and creating API's in Python. Recently, I started volunteering for the Malawi project, which is an open source project to develop a mobile application to help locals in Malawi find safe water for drinking and washing. I am the lead developer for this project, and am hoping it helps reduce disease in vulnerable areas. Outside of programming, I spend my spare time rollerskating, reading, and playing my saxophone :)
